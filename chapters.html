IEEE CS

The IEEE Computer Society is the world’s leading community of computing professionals and students dedicated to advancing computer science and technology through innovation, research, and collaboration. Through workshops, technical events, and knowledge-sharing initiatives, it empowers members to develop practical skills and stay updated with emerging technologies.

IEEE CIS

The IEEE Computational Intelligence Society (CIS) focuses on advancing intelligent systems inspired by nature and human intelligence. It covers areas such as artificial intelligence, neural networks, machine learning, fuzzy systems, and evolutionary computation. Through workshops, technical sessions, and collaborative projects, CIS encourages students to explore intelligent technologies and develop innovative solutions to real-world problems.

IEEE WIE

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is a global network dedicated to supporting and empowering women in engineering and technology. It aims to promote diversity, leadership, and professional growth within the engineering community. Through workshops, mentorship programs, and collaborative initiatives, WIE encourages students to develop technical skills, build confidence, and create opportunities for women to excel in STEM fields.

IEEE RAS

The IEEE Robotics and Automation Society (RAS) focuses on advancing robotics, automation, and intelligent systems that transform industries and everyday life. It promotes innovation in areas such as robotics, artificial intelligence, autonomous systems, and smart technologies. Through workshops, hands-on projects, and technical events, RAS provides students with opportunities to explore robotics technologies and develop practical skills in automation and intelligent systems.

IEEE PELS

The IEEE Power Electronics Society (PELS) is a global community dedicated to advancing power electronics technology and its applications. It promotes innovation in areas such as energy conversion, renewable energy systems, and efficient power management. Through workshops, technical talks, and collaborative initiatives, PELS empowers students to explore modern power electronics technologies and develop practical skills relevant to the evolving energy and electronics industry.

IEEE IAS

The IEEE Industry Applications Society (IAS) focuses on the advancement and practical application of electrical and electronic systems in industry. It brings together students and professionals to explore technologies used in industrial automation, energy systems, and electrical infrastructure. Through workshops, technical sessions, and industry-oriented initiatives, IAS helps members develop practical knowledge and skills while connecting them with real-world engineering applications.

IEEE CASS

The IEEE Circuits and Systems Society (CASS) focuses on advancing the theory, analysis, design, and practical implementation of circuits and systems. It plays a key role in areas such as signal processing, electronics, communication systems, and integrated circuit design. Through workshops, technical talks, and collaborative projects, CASS encourages students to explore innovative technologies and develop strong foundations in electronic and computational systems.
