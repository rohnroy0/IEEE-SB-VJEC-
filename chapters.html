IEEE CIS

It covers areas such as artificial intelligence, neural networks, machine learning, fuzzy systems, and evolutionary computation. Through workshops, technical sessions, and collaborative projects, CIS encourages students to explore intelligent technologies and develop innovative solutions to real-world problems.

IEEE WIE

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is a global network dedicated to supporting and empowering women in engineering and technology. It aims to promote diversity, leadership, and professional growth within the engineering community. Through workshops, mentorship programs, and collaborative initiatives, WIE encourages students to develop technical skills, build confidence, and create opportunities for women to excel in STEM fields.

IEEE SPS

The IEEE Signal Processing Society (SPS) focuses on advancing technologies related to the analysis, processing, and interpretation of signals such as audio, images, and data. It promotes innovation in areas like digital signal processing, machine learning, speech and image processing, and communication systems. Through workshops, technical sessions, and hands-on activities, SPS provides students with opportunities to explore modern signal processing techniques and develop practical skills in data analysis, multimedia processing, and intelligent signal-based applications.
