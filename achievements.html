Congratulations to Abdul Basith P V (S4 CSE-B) on being selected as the Web Master for the Student Leadership Kerala Team 2026 – IEEE LINK. Wishing him a successful and impactful journey ahead.

2026

IEEE Xplore Research Publication

The paper “Smart Rehab: Integrated IoT and Cloud-Enabled Physiotherapy System” by Abhinav V.M, Abhinav K, Yadunand Dharmajan, and Abin B. Krishnan, guided by Dr. Roshini T.V, has been successfully published in IEEE Xplore. A proud research milestone for Vimal Jyothi Engineering College.

2025

IEEE PES R10 – Event of the Month

The IoT Robotics & Drone Technology Internship, organized by IEEE PES SBC Vimal Jyothi Engineering College (VJEC) under the Kerala Section, has been recognized as the IEEE PES R10 “Event of the Month” for June 2025. This 10-day internship provided hands-on experience in IoT, robotics, and drone technology, empowering students with practical skills and real-world engineering insights. A proud achievement highlighting innovation, collaboration, and impactful learning.
