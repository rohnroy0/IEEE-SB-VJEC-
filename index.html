Who We Are

IEEE SB VJEC (IEEE Student Branch VJEC) is the official IEEE community of Vimal Jyothi Engineering College (VJEC).

We are a dynamic technical community focused on bridging the gap between academic learning and industry excellence. As a student branch under the world's largest technical professional organization, we provide our members with a platform to explore, innovate, and lead.

From organizing high-impact technical workshops and international conferences to fostering collaborative research projects, we empower students to transform their ideas into reality. Our community is built on the values of innovation, leadership, and technical advancement for the benefit of humanity.

Our Vision

To be a lighthouse of technical brilliance, illuminating the path for students to become global leaders who leverage technology for the betterment of humanity.

Our Mission

To cultivate an ecosystem of continuous learning and groundbreaking innovation, providing every student the tools, mentorship, and network to excel in the digital age.
